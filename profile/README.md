### What We Do

We help job seekers, remote workers, and expats answer the question everyone wonders but few can calculate: *"Is my salary actually good?"*

Our platform combines real salary data, progressive tax calculations, and cost-of-living indexes to give you an honest, numbers-backed answer — not just a gut feeling.

### Tools & Features

🧮 **Salary Calculator** — Calculate your take-home pay after taxes and deductions. Supports 11 countries with accurate progressive tax brackets.

📊 **Salary Percentile Checker** — See where your salary ranks compared to others in the same role and city. Are you in the top 10%? Top 50%? Find out.

🏙️ **Cost of Living Comparison** — Planning a move? Compare living costs between cities side by side — rent, groceries, transport, healthcare, and more.

🔐 **Cybersecurity Salary Tool** — Specialized salary benchmarks for cybersecurity roles, from SOC analyst to CISO.

📝 **Career Guides** — Data-driven articles on salary negotiation, career switching, and compensation trends.
